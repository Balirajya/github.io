How we are started

Why

In this era when science is developing so fast, most of our farmers are still using traditional ways of cultivation because of lack of knowledge, lack of resources and obviously because illiteracy. after all this it's very unfortunate to say that the most active group of people who get benefited without doing any hard work are supporting our farmers to be remain poor is known as middle man.

what we do?

what we do? we has a vision and mission of providing our farmers what they really deserve. How we will do it? we will help them to connect with their consumer directly without any middle man that they can get actual price of their work, we will educate them about new technology of cultivation that they can increase their production with same available Land and labor. we will provide them with best quality of seeds and compost that will help them to achieve their maximum production potential. and also by many other ways.

what you will get as a consumer?

1) low cost food products. 2) fresh and organically grown fruits. 3) Farm to your door step. 4) And satisfaction of helping people who actually help us.

How we are started

As a child while we were growing up, probably we all have heard the stories of poor farmers but very few of us rarely recognized that why always farmers are only poor why can't they be Rich. so same thought pop-up in our minds we were very curious that how can we make our farmers rich, how can we provide them maximum benefits of their work. our government is taking really great initiative to help them but as an responsible citizen it's also our responsibility to help our kishan.


Here is some data that we sharing with you to understand it better. as we all know that our culture is agriculture or we can say that 50-60% of Indian population is somehow depends upon agriculture and it has created 51% of employment for Indian population and aslo contribute 17-20% in indian GDP.


Now we all can recognize that how important agriculture is for us for our nation.


But if we will see the another side of coin it is really sad to say that who is satisfying our every days meals requirements are struggling to get their meals for one time.
